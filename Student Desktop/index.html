MythoMerMay 2019

MerMay is an art tradition on the internet where artists draw a mermaid a day in the month of May. This project I was a part of drew mermaids inspired by the greek women in mythology, goddess or nymph. By drawing a full piece every single day, it develops a habit for the artist in a healthy way.

Sadly, along with many other artists, I had to give up halfway due to my mental health and university work taking over all of my free time. I am proud of all the work I have done for the MerMay tradition, hopefully I am able to do it again next year.
